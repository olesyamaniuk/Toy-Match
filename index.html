How to Play

  • Find & Collect: 

    Select three identical 3D objects to clear them from the puzzle board.

  • Rotate & Sort:

    Move and rotate objects to get a clearer view of hidden matches.

  • Use Smart Boosters:

    Bombs, reshuffles, and hints help you clear tough levels effortlessly.

  • Advance Through Levels:

    Every puzzle introduces new challenges, from timed rounds to limited-move mechanics.

  • Build Your Toy Collection:

    Each level rewards you with beautifully detailed 3D toys that you can collect and showcase.

Pro Tip: Prioritize hard-to-reach objects and save your boosters for difficult challenges!

FAQ

  • What is Toy Match 3D: Triple Match?

    It's a 3D puzzle-matching game where players rotate, sort, and match objects to complete levels and collect unique toys.

  • How do I win a level?

    Successfully match all 3D objects before the board fills up or the timer runs out.

  • Can I play offline?

    Yes! Toy Match 3D supports offline play, making it the perfect game for travel, commutes, and relaxation.

  • What happens if I get stuck?

    Use boosters like reshuffles and bombs to clear obstacles and keep progressing.

  • How do I unlock new toys?

    Complete levels and participate in events to earn exclusive collectible toys!

  • Does the game get regular updates?

    Absolutely! Expect new levels, special events, and seasonal challenges in every update.

  • Is Toy Match 3D free-to-play?

    Yes! The game is 100% free, with optional in-game purchases for boosters and special items.

  • Can I challenge my friends?

    Yes! Compete in leaderboards and special events to prove who's the ultimate 3D matching master!
